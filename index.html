WELCOME

At Crema Espresso Bar and Café, we pride ourselves on using the finest ingredients available to craft a relaxing, cozy European café experience right here in Charlotte. We’re proud to feature delicious, locally roasted coffees and loose-leaf herbal teas paired with our European-inspired, made-from-scratch café food favorites, including our world-famous crepes.

All of our coffee and espresso drinks feature local Charlotte-area roasters who provide rich flavors from around the world and share our commitment to using only the finest ingredients. For tea lovers, Crema is proud to offer a rotating selection of approximately 30 varieties of the finest loose leaf herbal teas.

Our menu combines fresh, simple, and local ingredients with our family’s strong Eastern European culinary traditions providing delicious choices for whatever you’re looking for. We have options perfect for a snack, meal, or dessert. We’re certain you’ll love our crepes, pastries, cakes, and more incorporating familiar flavors with European flair.
